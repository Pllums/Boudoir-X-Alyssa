Hey there, I'm Alyssa

I am your new hype woman, nice to meet you. I’m a boudoir photographer located in Grand Rapids, Michigan. I have quickly found that boudoir is my calling. Right away I saw the empowerment and confidence it brought the women I was working with. I started to appreciate that all bodies are different and still just as beautiful. My entire life I have struggled with body image and comparison to others, never finding what I had to be good enough. Boudoir has given me a whole new view on that. Helping others feel beautiful enough, young enough, confident enough and perfectly acceptable exactly where they are TODAY pushes me to take that same advice. Every woman I have worked with has chosen boudoir for different reasons. Whether it be for their partner, celebrating a mile stone, or helping them find their confidence. What ever the reason may be I have seen a tremendous impact on how they view themselves, and I can’t wait to help YOU. I look forward to working with you. If you have any questions feel free to ask away over on my contact page. xoxo

-Alyssa Abdullah
