FAQs

Can I bring someone with me to the shoot?

You are more than welcome to bring 1 friend along. They must be supportive and positive. If they are not I will ask them to leave.

Can I afford this?

Absolutely. I want to work with you and make sure you are able to afford/budget this experience, so I do offer payment plans if needed.
Payments must be made in full before you will receive images.

What is required booking?

I require a retainer fee of $150 day of booking.
This retainer locks in your studio session and your overall session cost. This is non refundable under any circumstances.

Where will we be shooting?

All sessions take place at my studio located at 1111 Godfrey Ave SW, Grand Rapids, MI. It's a beautiful well lit industrial meets luxury space just waiting for you to come fill it with your gorgeous face.

Do you have a client closet?

Yes, all my pieces are modeled on my client closet tab. Sizes range from small-2xl currently

Do you share my images?

Not without permission. I do not share any images without my clients full consent and a signed model release form. I offer three different sharing options: 1 being your eyes only, 2nd is anonymous images only or 3rd being a full share. All options are completely okay with me, after all these are your images!
